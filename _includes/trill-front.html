Trill Touch Sensors

Trill is a family of touch sensors that was inspired by the way musicians use their hands to interact with the world. Compatible with Bela as well as platforms like Arduino, Teensy and Raspberry Pi, Trill sensors use capacitive sensing to precisely track finger movements and positions.
